Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password?
    You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your email.
  • Can I track multiple habits at once?
    Yes! You can create and track multiple habits simultaneously. There's no limit to the number of habits you can track.
  • How do I join the community?
    The community is automatically available to all registered users. Just create an account and start engaging with others!
  • Is my data secure?
    Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your personal data and habit information.
