Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new habit to track?
Currently, you can track three predefined habits: Exercising, Drinking Water, and Reading. We're working on adding custom habit creation in a future update.
Can I change the time range for my progress view?
Yes! You can switch between "Last 7 Days", "Last 30 Days", and "All Time" views using the time range buttons at the top of the dashboard.
How is my completion rate calculated?
Your completion rate is calculated as the percentage of days you've completed your habit out of the total days in the selected time range.
Is my data stored securely?
Yes, all your habit data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any personal information on our servers.
Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Currently, the app works independently on each device. We're developing sync functionality that will be available in a future update.
