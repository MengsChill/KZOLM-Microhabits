Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with microhabits?

Start small! Choose one tiny habit that takes less than 2 minutes to complete. Attach it to an existing routine and track your consistency. Our "Getting Started" guide has more detailed instructions.

Is there a mobile app for Kzolm?

Yes! We have mobile apps for both iOS and Android. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play Store. The apps sync with your web account so you can track habits anywhere.

Do you offer personalized habit coaching?

We offer personalized recommendations through our platform, and our premium members get access to one-on-one habit coaching sessions. Contact us to learn more about our premium offerings.

Can I use Kzolm with my team or organization?

Absolutely! We offer team and enterprise plans that include group tracking, shared habits, and administrator tools. Contact our sales team at enterprise@kzolm.com for more information.
